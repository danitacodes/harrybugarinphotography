My Work

My journey into photography started in 2020 during the pandemic.
I was always interested in photography but never took the steps to get into it.
That year, I decided to purchase my first professional camera
coupled with a few lenses for my kit and was off shooting the following day.

What first started out as a hobby quickly became my passion.

I have done events, product photography, lifestyle photography, fashion photography,
but have found the most satisfaction in portrait photography and in sports photography.
